Example (Mario)

Win Condition: Get a grab or up-tilt at low/mid %, push to platforms, then ledge trap or edgeguard. Kill with up-smash, back-air, or fair spikes.

Key Neutral Tools: Fireball for approach/conditioning, short hop nair, back-air, dash grab.

Bread-and-Butter: d-throw → uair chains, up-tilt strings at low %, nair → up-smash as a kill confirm.

Knowing your character deeply is a shortcut to consistency in bracket.
