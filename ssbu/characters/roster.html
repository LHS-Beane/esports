Super Smash Bros. Ultimate — Competitive Character Roster

Varsity-level character breakdowns for Louisville High School Esports

Banjo & Kazooie

Role Summary: Mid-range zoner with burst options, strong survivability, and limited but powerful resources.

Key Normals

  • Nair: Fast get-off-me tool; great defensive aerial.
  • Fair: Strong kill move; slow but devastating.
  • Bair: Quick multi-hit; great for spacing.
  • Uair: Combo finisher and juggle tool.
  • Dair: Double downward hit; risky but powerful spike.
  • FTilt: Disjointed kick for spacing.
  • UTilt: Anti-air with good KO power.
  • DTilt: Trip setups into grab.

Specials

  • Neutral B — Egg Firing: Zoning tool; can be used as pressure.
  • Side B — Wonderwing: Limited armor burst; kills extremely early.
  • Up B — Shock Spring Jump: Vertical recovery; trap setups possible.
  • Down B — Rear Egg: Grenade that enables combos and ledge traps.

Kill Moves

Fair, UTilt, Wonderwing, FSmash, Uair, Side-B (low %).

Out-of-Shield Options

Nair (fastest), Grab, Wonderwing (reads), Uair (if opponent is above).

Advantage Tools

Grenade traps, Uair juggling, ledge trapping with egg + bair.

Recovery Notes

Strong vertical recovery; mix jump + Up B. Wonderwing can bypass edgeguards.

Beginner → Advanced Tech

  • Basic: Grenade → grab confirms.
  • Intermediate: Egg juggle extensions.
  • Advanced: Grenade drop combos, platform tech traps.
Bayonetta

Role Summary: Air combo specialist with strong mobility, edgeguarding, and ladder strings. Weak kill power without confirms.

Key Normals

  • Nair: Quick multi-hit; safe pressure tool.
  • Fair: Combo part + edgeguard finisher.
  • Bair: Strong kill move.
  • Uair: Main ladder tool.
  • Dair: Downward heel drop—spike hitbox.
  • Tilts: Used to start strings and punish landings.

Specials

  • Neutral B — Bullet Arts: Chip damage; cancels landing lag.
  • Side B — After Burner Kick: Main combo starter.
  • Up B — Witch Twist: Vertical combo extender.
  • Down B — Witch Time: Counter that slows opponent.

Kill Moves

Bair, DSmash, ABK → Fair, Witch Time punishes, Uair at high %.

Out-of-Shield Options

Witch Twist (frame 4), Up Tilt, Nair.

Advantage Tools

Ladder combos, offstage fair strings, ABK frame traps.

Recovery Notes

Multiple specials allow huge distance but predictable paths.

Beginner → Advanced Tech

  • Basic: ABK → Uair.
  • Intermediate: Double ABK extensions.
  • Advanced: DI tracking ladders, ABK platform resets.
Bowser

Role Summary: Heavyweight grappler with massive kill power, strong OOS options, and terrifying pressure.

Key Normals

  • Nair: Great combo breaker.
  • Fair: Strong aerial that kills early.
  • Bair: One of his best kill moves.
  • Uair: Anti-air KO option.
  • Dair: Drop kick; punish tool.
  • FTilt: Insane power; ledge-tool.
  • Jab: Frame 4; good pressure.

Specials

  • Neutral B — Flame Breath: 0–50% damage builder.
  • Side B — Flying Slam: Command grab that kills early.
  • Up B — Whirling Fortress: Frame 6 OOS option.
  • Down B — Bowser Bomb: Hard read option.

Kill Moves

Bair, Side-B, Uair, FSmash, FTilt, Fair.

Out-of-Shield Options

Up B (frame 6), Nair, Grab.

Advantage Tools

Flame breath ledge trap, FTilt at ledge, Uair juggles.

Recovery Notes

Predictable but armor helps. Mix drift + Up-B angles.

Beginner → Advanced Tech

  • Basic: Flame breath → grab.
  • Intermediate: Tomahawk Side-B.
  • Advanced: Platform Side-B kills, DI mixups.
