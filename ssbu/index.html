Player Expectations

Practice Standards

  • • Use drills, not only friendlies.
  • • Have a goal every session (movement, ledge trapping, etc.).
  • • Track at least one thing you improved each week.
  • • Bring one question from your practice to the coach.

Competitive Standards

  • • Show respect to teammates and opponents.
  • • Take responsibility for your habits and mindset.
  • • Watch and review your own VODs regularly.
  • • Support teammates in learning matchups and prep.
